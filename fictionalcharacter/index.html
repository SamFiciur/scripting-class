Luz Noceda

About

Luz Noceda is the main protagonist in the Disney Channel show The Owl House. She is a latina human teenager who gets teleported to a magical world called the Boiling Ilses in the Demon Realm. She trains under Eda the Owl Lady to become a witch even though humans cannot use magic. She attends a school for witches called Hexside with her friends to learn more about magic.
